How it Works

Location, Location, Location...what to plant where?

  • Click on the "Location" tab in the header.
    (Make "nature of the location" choices that apply to you and press the "submit" button. 3 plant options will appear for your considerations).
  • Go to the "Local Nurseries" tab and find a local nursery near you to to purchase these plants.

That's it. Have fun and happy planting.
